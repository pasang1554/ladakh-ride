15 Must-Visit Destinations in Leh-Ladakh

By Ladakh Ride Team • 6 min read

From the azure waters of Pangong to the sand dunes of Hunder, Ladakh is a land of contrasts. Here is our curated list of the top 15 places that define the magic of this high-altitude desert.

1. Pangong Tso

The world-famous color-changing lake. A night stay here offers some of the best stargazing opportunities.

2. Nubra Valley (Hunder)

Ride the double-humped Bactrian camels on the sand dunes, surrounded by snowy peaks.

3. Thiksey Monastery

Resembling the Potala Palace in Lhasa, this is one of the grandest monasteries in Ladakh.

4. Khardung La Pass

One of the highest motorable roads in the world. A photo here is a rite of passage for every biker and traveler.

5. Tso Moriri

A pristine, less-crowded alternative to Pangong. It’s part of a wetland reserve and is stunningly beautiful.

6. Magnetic Hill

A gravity hill where vehicles appear to defy the laws of physics.

7. Sangam Point

The confluence of the Indus and Zanskar rivers, showing distinct colors meeting.

8. Shanti Stupa

A white-domed stupa on a hilltop in Chanspa, offering panoramic views of Leh city.

9. Leh Palace

A former royal palace overlooking the town of Leh.

10. Hemis National Park

Home to the elusive Snow Leopard.

(...and 5 more: Alchi, Lamayuru, Hanle, Turtuk, and Warila Pass)
